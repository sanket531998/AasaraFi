Etherlink hackathon - summer of code 2025

Project Name: AasaraFi
Concept: A platform where individuals in underserved regions can collateralize physical IoT-enabled goods (e.g., solar panels, water purifiers, motorcycles) registered as NFTs and use them to secure microloans

Frontend: NextJS and tailwind (mobile first)
Backend: NodeJS & Express

Target Audience
AasaraFi targets mobile-first users in emerging markets with limited access to decentralized finance infrastructure.
We serve non-crypto-native users by abstracting away wallet complexity, enabling seamless onboarding through smart accounts and social authentication.
